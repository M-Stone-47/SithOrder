Darth Sidious

Real Name: Sheev Palpatine

Age: 119

Master: Not known in Canon

Darth Sidious is The Secret Sith Identity of the chancellor of the Galactic Republic Palpatine. He orchestrated the clone wars to destabilize the Galactic Republic and thus getting more power from the Senate.

In the end of the clone wars a Jedi Master called Mace Windu tried to arrest him for being a Sith but he was rescued by the Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker and killed the Jedi Master. After that executed Order 66 which marked all jedi as Traitors and to be executed at sight. Together with his apprentice Lord Vader he hunted the Jedi almost to Extinction and Reorganized the Republic into the first Galactic Empire which he ruled for almost 23 years before getting killed by his Apprentice and Luke Skywalker.

After his "Death" he Transferred his Spirit in a Clone of himself on Exegol and planned his revenge on the galaxy. meanwhile one of his clones ran away, got married and got a daughter which he named Rey which would grow up without her parents on a desert planet called Jakku and oneday becomes a Jedi. Sidious Meanwhile used one of his clones to create the First Order and Declared war on the new republic. after a few years he finnally was ready to announce to the Galaxy that he was alive and that the day of revenge was soon there.

After that the resistance came to exegol where he and his new fleet waited. while the fleets battled he, rey and Ben Solo fought until he realised he could use their power to regenerate his broken body and so the power of two helped restore the one true Emperor of the sith. But after a few minutes Rey stud up and killed the emperor with the help from the ghosts of all Jedi
