Darth Sidious

Real Name: Sheev Palpatine

Home planet: Naboo

Age at death: 119

Master: Not known in Canon

This might be one of the most powerful Sith lords and force user in Star Wars history, he was extremely Talented with the force. But he also mastered every Lightsaber style there is, even though he hated lightsaber and would much rather torture his Enemy with his force lighting. He even bested the most powerful of Jedi of his era, like master Yoda.

Darth Sidious is The Secret Sith Identity of the chancellor of the Galactic Republic Palpatine. He orchestrated the clone wars to destabilize the Galactic Republic, thus getting more power from the Senate and slowly seed distrust among the people of the Republic and the Jedi

In the end of the clone wars a Jedi Master called Mace Windu found out the fact that he was the hidden Sith lord the Jedi searched for and tried to arrest him for being a Sith but he was rescued by the Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker and killed the Jedi Master. After that executed Order 66, which marked all jedi as Traitors and to be executed at sight, and took Anakin Skywalker as his new Sith apprentice. Together with his apprentice Lord Vader he hunted the Jedi almost to Extinction and Reorganized the Republic into the first Galactic Empire which he ruled as Emperor Palpatine for almost 23 years before getting killed by his Apprentice and Luke Skywalker.

After his "Death" he Transferred his Spirit in a Clone of himself on exegol and planned his revenge on the galaxy. meanwhile one of his clones ran away, got married and got a daughter which he named Rey which would grow up without her parents on a desert planet called Jakku and one day becomes a Jedi. Sidious Meanwhile used one of his clones, called Snoke, to create the First Order and Declared war on the new Republic. after a few years he finally was ready to announce to the Galaxy that he was alive and that the day of his revenge would come soon.

After that the resistance came to exegol where he and his new fleet of the "Last Order" waited. while the fleets battled he, Rey and Ben Solo fought until he realised he could use their power to regenerate his broken body and so the power of two helped restore the one true Emperor of the Sith. He Unleashed his full furry on the fleet of the resistance, deactivating all their electronics with his lightning, But after a few minutes Rey stud up and killed the emperor with the help from the ghosts of all Jedi and saved the galaxy form his wrath.
