Darth Tyranus

Real Name: Count Dooku

Age at death: 83

Master: Darth Sidious

Count Dooku once was a Jedi master with a seat in the high council and was the padawan of grandmaster Yoda, Dooku also was one of the Greatest Duelist in the entire Jedi order. He was the master of Qui Gon Jinn and almost considert him his son, so when Qui Gon died he left the Jedi Order and Experimented with the Dark side of the force until Sidious Found him and took him as his new Sith apprentice.

He ordered the Clone army for the Republic and created the Separatist movement Called C.I.S (Confederation of Independent System) by order of Sidious to Destabilize the Republic and starting the clone wars. The C.I.S declared war on the Republic to gain their Independent believing the Republic would have no way to fight back and was surprised to see the new clone army of the Republic.

During the clone wars he was mostly on Diplomatic missions to get other System to join the C.I.S and training his own Assassin Ajajj Ventress, who he had to kill to prof his loyalty to Sidious who feared that Dooku tries overthrowing him with Ventress.

In the Battle of Corucant he and his General kidnapped the Chancellor. He was ordered by Sidious to toy with Anakin, Luring him to the dark side, but after the fight he was defeated and Killed by the Jedi knight Anakin Skywalker who was told to kill him by Palpatine
